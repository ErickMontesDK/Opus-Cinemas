About Us

Where Code Meets the Silver Screen

At Opus Cinemas, we’re redefining the moviegoing experience in the heart of Vancouver, B.C. Born as a passion project for students at the Canadian College of Technology and Business (CCTB), we combine cutting-edge technology with the timeless joy of cinema to create unforgettable moments for film lovers.

Our Vision

We believe movies are more than entertainment—they’re a gateway to new worlds. That’s why we’ve designed Opus Cinemas as a tech-forward destination where innovation enhances every frame. From seamless online ticketing (crafted by our very own CCTB developers) to immersive audiovisual experiences, we’re building the future of cinema, one credit at a time.

How We're Different

  • Smart Database Backbone: Our Supabase-powered system securely stores seat bookings and payment details, ensuring real-time accuracy without users ever seeing the complexity.
  • Dynamic Movie Carousel: Know exactly what's playing now or coming soon through our auto-updating display, powered by live API integrations.
  • Visual Seat Selection: Our interactive auditorium map lets you claim your perfect spot with one click, with instant reservation updates.

Behind the Scenes (2025 Edition)

This project began when CCTB students asked: "How can we build a cinema platform that's as functional as it is educational?" What emerged was a full-stack marvel featuring:

  • MovieGlu API Integration: Pulls real-time showtimes and film details so you never see outdated info.
  • Smart Recommendations: Our algorithm highlights popular films based on actual booking data—no guesswork.
  • Student-Built: Every line of code reflects CCTB's hands-on learning philosophy.

Join Us Whether you’re a Vancouver local, a CCTB student, or just someone who loves movies with a side of ingenuity, Opus Cinemas welcomes you. Together, we’re scripting the next reel in cinematic history.
